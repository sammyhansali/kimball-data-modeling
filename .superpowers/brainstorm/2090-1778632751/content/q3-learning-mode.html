How should the learning flow work?

You're following the Kimball 4-step process — what's your role vs. mine?

A

You design, I guide (Recommended)

I pose the Kimball questions ("what's the grain?", "which dimensions?"). You answer and make the decisions. I give feedback aligned to what the book says and explain why

B

I design, you implement

I walk through the design decisions chapter-style and explain them, then you write the dbt models. More guided, less open-ended

C

Collaborative back-and-forth

We discuss each decision together — I share the Kimball reasoning, you push back or extend it, then we implement together
